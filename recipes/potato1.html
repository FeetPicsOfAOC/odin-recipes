DEEP FRIED POTATO

Description

It's a DEEP FRIED POTATO! Very good and delicious. Not as healthy as other potatoes, but it's the most tasty dish ever.

Ingridients

  • Potato
  • Oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Slice the potato. It's temting to eat the potato right now, I know, but bear with me!!
  2. Submerge the potato slices into the oil. Cook until beatiful brown color!! Don't over do it!
  3. Add salt while it's still hot and greasy!!
