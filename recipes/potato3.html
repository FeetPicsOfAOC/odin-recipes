MASHED POTATO

Description

Mashed potatoes are the healthiest!! Well, it depends on how much butter you will add...

Ingridients

  • Potato
  • Water
  • Parsley

Steps

  1. Boil the potato until tender!! Very important step!
  2. MASH THE POTATO!! Like it's the last thing you will ever do! Mash it good with a spoon or fork!
  3. Chop parsley and add this beautiful green to the potato! Now it's not only delicious, but beautiful too!!!
